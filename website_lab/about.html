About the AquaShield Research Lab website

This website was designed as a polished academic showcase and as a practical environment for cybersecurity experiments involving behavioral bot detection.

Why this site exists

This demo site serves two goals at the same time. First, it gives you a visually strong project website with multiple polished pages, cards, sections, and images. Second, it creates a realistic navigation graph that can be logged and analyzed by your web scraping detector.

Because the site includes category browsing, article reading, search, detail pages, and form interactions, it is useful for collecting richer browsing sessions from both humans and bots.
